# 一个创业者的祛魅之路

## Film Direction

Palette system: warm paper canvas dominates; black ink carries structure and body copy; muted red is reserved for reversals, stamps, active numbers, and the single changing protagonist in a diagram. Secondary colors stay quiet and material-like, never neon.

Type roles: display type is used for one large concept per scene; body text is sparse and subordinate; mono text is only for version numbers, counters, and labels that need a measured feeling.

Motion defaults + budget: each scene gets one macro movement plus one or two live secondary elements. Entries use `EASE.entry`; emphasis uses `EASE.emphasis`; ambient motion uses `EASE.drift` only when the scene needs a living hold. Avoid exit tweens before transitions; scenes should reach a readable hero frame and let the harness transition carry the cut.

Ambient system: paper grain, light grid, ink-line frames, small pinned details, and subtle offset shadows recur across the film. The frame should feel like a careful notebook argument, not a startup pitch deck.

Negative list: no real product screenshots, no website capture, no generic AI glow, no purple-blue tech gradients, no dense dashboards, no decorative bokeh, no stock-photo startup imagery.

Transition vocabulary: use `crossfade`, `blur-crossfade`, `push-slide`, and `zoom-through` only. Continue runs keep the same visual stage and evolve the carried object in one worker.

Visual register mix: scenes 1-2 are kinetic metaphor and abstract pressure; scene 3 is timeline/type; scenes 4-5 are process and score transformation; scene 6 is simple data proof; scene 7 is spatial analogy; scenes 8-9 are diagram-to-closing editorial image.

Stillness-before-climax allocation: scene 6 holds after the PMF balloon collapses into cashflow; scene 9 holds on the final line.

Captions: transcript captions occupy the lower band; primary graphics stay above the caption territory unless a scene intentionally gives the caption the final word.

## Scene 1: 先祛魅

**Effects:** [`kinetic-beat-slam`, `physics-press-reaction`, `sine-wave-loop`]
**Duration:** 6.18s
**Continuity:** break
**Transition:** crossfade 0.4s
**Hierarchy:** simple

Surprised but plain, like opening a notebook with an uncomfortable sentence already underlined. Center the word “创业” as the primary display object; financing, hiring, and office symbols sit around it as small inflated bubbles. The red “祛魅” stamp lands only after the bubbles feel slightly too big, compressing the whole cluster into a calmer, flatter arrangement. The eye should leave on the compressed pressure shapes, ready to become measurable indicators in the next scene.

## Scene 2: 大厂毒素

**Effects:** [`stat-bars-and-fills`, `svg-path-draw`, `counting-dynamic-scale`, `sine-wave-loop`]
**Duration:** 8.36s
**Continuity:** continue
**Hierarchy:** simple

Tense and self-aware, with the same pressure now turning into numbers and gauges. The inflated bubbles from scene 1 become three vertical gauges: 融资, 团队, 办公室. Each rises past a reasonable mark, then thin root-like lines draw out of a head outline to make the “毒素” metaphor visible without becoming grotesque. Keep the carried subject as the pressure indicator family; by the end, the gauges should feel overgrown enough that cutting to a small version number feels like relief.

## Scene 3: 版本号就是态度

**Effects:** [`discrete-text-sequence`, `css-marker-patterns`, `scale-swap-transition`]
**Duration:** 7s
**Continuity:** break
**Transition:** blur-crossfade 0.5s
**Hierarchy:** simple

Quiet and precise, a reset from expansion to honesty. Use a horizontal version ruler from 0.1 to 1.0; 1.0 is present but distant, dotted, or locked, while 0.1 receives the active red underline. The scene should not look like software release notes; it should read as a moral posture expressed through a tiny product detail. The eye lands on 0.1, which becomes the first step of a product ladder in the next scene.

## Scene 4: 四步产品观

**Effects:** [`vertical-spring-ticker`, `card-morph-anchor`, `svg-icon-enrichment`, `sine-wave-loop`]
**Duration:** 7.36s
**Continuity:** break
**Transition:** push-slide LEFT 0.45s
**Hierarchy:** simple

Curious and ordered, like watching a product philosophy assemble itself. Stack four steps vertically: 好看, 好用, 好卖, 好玩. The first step is not just highlighted; it opens like a doorway, implying “without desire to open, nothing else matters.” Keep labels large and sparse. The active first step should remain visually available at the end so the next scene can transform it into a quality score.

## Scene 5: 从快到好

**Effects:** [`counting-dynamic-scale`, `card-morph-anchor`, `css-marker-patterns`, `svg-path-draw`]
**Duration:** 10.18s
**Continuity:** continue
**Hierarchy:** simple

Momentum and a small aha, turning “好看” into “作品变好”. The doorway/step becomes a score card: 30/100 improves toward 80/100 as polish layers, pencil corrections, and alignment marks appear. A tiny clock icon can shrink away, but do not make speed the protagonist. The primary subject is the improving artifact; supporting labels only explain the shift from saving time to raising quality. End with the polished card stable enough to cut into a business metric.

## Scene 6: PMF 祛魅

**Effects:** [`physics-press-reaction`, `stat-bars-and-fills`, `counting-dynamic-scale`]
**Duration:** 7.5s
**Continuity:** break
**Transition:** crossfade 0.35s
**Hierarchy:** simple

Skeptical and grounding, the most blunt business beat. A shiny PMF balloon enters, gets punctured, and resolves into a simple cashflow line labeled around “3-6 months” and “养活团队”. Keep the graphic almost embarrassingly plain; that plainness is the point. After the pop, hold the line chart long enough for the definition to feel practical rather than clever. The final line becomes a thin plane for the area-versus-volume metaphor.

## Scene 7: 体积不是面积

**Effects:** [`split-tilt-cards`, `3d-text-depth-layers`, `scale-swap-transition`, `sine-wave-loop`]
**Duration:** 8.66s
**Continuity:** break
**Transition:** zoom-through 0.45s
**Hierarchy:** simple

Clear and spatial, a simple geometry argument. Show one wide, thin plane for 通用 Agent and one smaller but thicker block for 内容创作. The flat plane should feel impressive but fragile; the cube should feel modest but durable. Avoid making this a competitive product chart. The eye should follow the cube’s depth as it breaks into nodes for the Skills-market flywheel.

## Scene 8: 诚实的飞轮

**Effects:** [`avatar-cloud-network`, `svg-path-draw`, `orbit-3d-entry`, `sine-wave-loop`]
**Duration:** 9.22s
**Continuity:** break
**Transition:** push-slide LEFT 0.45s
**Hierarchy:** simple

Forward-looking but not triumphant. The thick cube separates into three nodes: 技能创作者, 技能市场, 内容创作者. A single red path draws the flywheel, then small dots circulate to show creators selling experience and the platform growing through people helping people. Keep the cycle readable and sparse; no marketplace UI mockup. Let the orbit slow near the end so the final scene can dissolve into a human desk.

## Scene 9: 诚实的人

**Effects:** [`discrete-text-sequence`, `css-marker-patterns`, `sine-wave-loop`]
**Duration:** 10.588s
**Continuity:** continue
**Hierarchy:** simple

Resolved and quiet, with the least motion in the film. The flywheel dissolves into a minimal desk and a small system message: “要不看看窗外，放松一下”. Then the final sentence types in as the only primary object: “诚实的人，诚实的产品。” Let the last line hold without extra flourish. The ending should feel earned, not advertised.
